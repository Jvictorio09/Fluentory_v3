Frequently asked questions

Quick answers about access, exams, payments, and support.

Access & devices

How long do I have access to the course?

You get lifetime access. You can log in anytime, from any device, with no expiry date on your content.

Can I learn on my phone or tablet?

Yes. Lessons work on desktop, tablet, and mobile, and you can download PDFs and audio for offline study.

Exams & certificates

How do the exams work?

Each course includes quizzes and a final exam. You can retake if needed, and your progress is saved as you go.

Is the certificate recognized?

Certificates are issued by Fluentory and underpinned by our accreditation partners listed above.

Payments & refunds

What payment methods do you accept?

You can pay securely with major credit/debit cards. Additional payment options may be available at checkout.

How do refunds work?

If the program isn’t right for you within 30 days, contact support and we’ll process a full refund.

Troubleshooting

What if I have a technical issue?

Our support team is here to help. Reach out via email and we’ll get you back into your course quickly.

Can I switch courses?

If you enroll and realize another path is a better fit, contact us within 30 days and we’ll help you move.
