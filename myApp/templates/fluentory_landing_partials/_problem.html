THE REAL REASON

Why most students never become fluent

It’s rarely about intelligence or effort. It’s the method: you train knowledge, but you never train speaking under real pressure.

  • Endless grammar drills that don’t translate into real conversation.

  • Fear of mistakes keeps you quiet when it matters most.

  • No safe space to practice — without judgment or embarrassment.

  • Learning in isolation — disconnected from real-world situations.

FLUENTORY fixes this by training conversation first — with structure, feedback, and repetition.
